About Me

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm Moaz Mahmoud Sabra, a computer science graduate with an insatiable curiosity for all things tech and life.

My passion ignited young – since I was 14, I've loved to build things and break them, especially in the realm of technology. This fascination naturally led me to cybersecurity, where I've developed a deep love for penetration testing and red teaming. While I'm currently honing my skills in backend development with Django and Python – creating robust digital foundations – my compass is set firmly on transitioning into cybersecurity, aiming to explore every domain from offensive to defensive security, and from application to hardware and IoT security. My ultimate aspiration? To build a mind-blowing startup that pushes boundaries.

Beyond the lines of code and security exploits, my world expands into the rich tapestry of Arabic poetry and literature. I find immense joy in reading heritage and modern Arabic books, delving into Islamic history, Quranic, and Hadith studies. And yes, I also have a soft spot for horses, art, and everything beautiful that sparks inspiration. A particular fondness of mine is also tasting coffee, especially the rich aroma and tradition of Arabic coffee.

This blog is where these worlds collide. You'll find my insights into the ever-evolving tech landscape, deep dives into cybersecurity, alongside reflections on literature, history, the beauty I encounter in everyday life, and perhaps even a note on a great cup of coffee. Join me as I build, break, learn, and explore!
